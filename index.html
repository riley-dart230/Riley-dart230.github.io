Morning Harmony

In the morning light, the sky is blue,

The flowers bloom with drops of dew.

The birds allsing a cheerful song,

And all the world seems bright and strong.


The trees sway gently in the breeze,

Their leaves rustling with such ease.

The sun climbs high, a golden ball,

And warmth and jpy for all do call.


In simple moments, peace will find,

A gentle calm for heart and mind.
